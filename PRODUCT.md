# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Gym-goers tracking workouts on their phones, typically in-gym between sets. They need quick, easy, single-tap entry, clean readability under gym lighting, and simple navigation.

## Product Purpose
LiftLog is an offline workout tracker that allows users to track their sets, reps, weight logs, YouTube exercises guides, calendars, and progress without a backend. It exists to simplify workout tracking and help users visualize their performance trends.

## Brand Personality
Focused, energetic, high-performance.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic SaaS-monochrome layouts, beige/cream palettes, complex multi-step navigation, and cluttered interfaces. It should not look like a generic dashboard.

## Design Principles
1. **Utility-First Speed**: Design for fast entry. Logging a set should take minimum taps/keystrokes when the user is tired or between sets.
2. **High Glanceability**: High-contrast, clear typography readable under overhead gym lighting or on a phone screen lying on the floor.
3. **Focused Progression**: Make personal records, streaks, and progress feel rewarding and highly visible without distracting from the workout.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability under strong gym lights.
- Large tap targets (≥48px) for sweaty fingers.
- Support for reduced motion preferences.
